New Cockaparents

So you have decided to get a Cockapoo puppy, what an exciting decision! They are a lively medium-sized dog with a lot of energy. On the outside those teddy bear looks make them appear to be incredibly sweet and fluffy, and yes they are that too, but do not be fooled; they are super busy dogs who will find lots of trouble if their inquisitive nature is not properly channelled!
Your new Cockapoo will require training like all dogs, but also because they are a mix of two working breeds, the Cocker Spaniel and the Miniature Poodle. You can start to train your Cockapoo puppy as soon as you get them home. Cockapoos are super smart and are always learning, so start by simply rewarding them with treats, play and praise when they do the right thing

To have the most fun with your new best friend, it is a good idea to master the following areas:

  • Sitting
  • Lying down
  • Learning their name
  • Coming when you call them
  • Walking nicely on a lead
  • Playing fetch
  • Sleeping in their crate
  • Coping with being left alone
  • Going to the toilet in the right place
